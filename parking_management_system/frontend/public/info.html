How to Use

1. Finding Available Slots

You can find available parking slots in two ways:

  • Use the Chat Assistant and ask about available slots
  • Go to the Available Slots page and use the filters

Example Chat Query:

"Show me available car parking slots at Phoenix Mall for tomorrow at 3 PM for 2 hours"

2. Booking a Slot

You can book a parking slot in two ways:

  • Through the Chat Assistant by providing all required details
  • Directly from the Available Slots page by clicking the "Book" button

Required information for booking:

  • Mall name
  • Vehicle type (car, bike, or truck)
  • Date and time
  • Duration (in hours)
  • Vehicle license plate number

3. Managing Bookings

You can manage your bookings in two ways:

  • Through the Chat Assistant by asking to view or cancel bookings
  • From the Bookings page where you can view and cancel bookings

Example Chat Query:

"Show my bookings" or "Cancel booking 12"
